Features

Everything your pantry needs. Nothing it doesn't.

Spichr is built around the things that actually matter in a kitchen — expiry dates, quick access, and no clutter.

🔔

Expiry Notifications

Get notified before items expire. Customize how far in advance you want reminders, per category or globally.

☁️

iCloud Sync

Your pantry syncs seamlessly across all your Apple devices via CloudKit — automatically and privately.

📱

Home Screen Widgets

Check upcoming expiry dates at a glance, right from your Home Screen. Built with WidgetKit.

🛒

Shopping Lists

Build shopping lists based on what you're running low on. Organize by store section or custom order.

🎙️

Siri Shortcuts

Add items, check your pantry, or start your shopping list with your voice using App Intents.

📤

JSON Export

Export your complete pantry data as JSON at any time — for backup, migration, or your own analysis.

🔍

Advanced Filtering

Sort and filter by category, location, expiry date, or tags. Find anything in your pantry instantly.

🍳

Recipe Suggestions

Get recipe ideas based on what you actually have on hand — especially useful before items expire.

🌍

18 Languages

Fully localized for a global audience. English, German, French, Spanish, Japanese, and 13 more.

🔒

Your data stays yours.

Spichr is built without advertising, without analytics, and without collecting personal information. Your pantry is private by default.

✓ No account required ✓ No ads or tracking ✓ No third-party analytics ✓ Optional iCloud sync ✓ Full data export
